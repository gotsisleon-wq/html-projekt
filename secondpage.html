Chops for warriors

  1. 1 Tablespoon dried thyme
  2. 1 Tablespoon dried rosemary
  3. 1 Teaspoon table salt
  4. 180ml Extra virgin olive oil
  5. 1 1/4kg rack of lamb
  6. 60ml date syrup

Instructions

Preheat oven to 160Â°C
Mix thyme, rosemary, salt and olive oil.
Rub lamb rack with half the marinade, reserve the other half.
Add date syrup to the reserved half of the marinade and whisk well to combine. Set aside.
In a skillet over high heat, sear the lamb for 1 minute on each side.
Transfer lamb to a baking dish and brush with marinade. Roast for around 40 minutes in the middle of the oven. Once half the cooking time has passed, brush with marinade again.
When lamb is done roasting, take it out of the oven and brush it with marinade yet again.
Allow the meat to rest for about 5 minutes before serving.
